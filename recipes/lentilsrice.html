Lentils and Rice

Description

One pot lentils and rice. It's ok. Makes a lot.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lentils
  • 2 cups rice
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Wash lentils and add to large pot with water to cover lentils by 1/2 inch.
  2. Simmer lentils for 20 minutes, until most of the water has been absorbed.
  3. Add rice and 3.5 cups of water. Simmer for 20 minutes, until all water absorbed.
  4. Save 4 cups for people who want unseasoned.
  5. Mix in spices and let set for 5 to 10 minutes.

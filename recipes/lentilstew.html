Lentil Stew

Description

Sausage and lentil stew for you.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs dried lentils
  • 1 lbs smoked sausage
  • 5 carrots, chopped
  • 8 c water
  • 3 tbs oil
  • Spices of your choice

Steps

  1. Saute chopped sausage in oil on high heat.
  2. Add washed lentils, carrots, water, spices.
  3. Simmer for 1 hour or until desired thickness.

Beans and Rice

Description

This is like a bean soup. Serve it on rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag 16 bean soup, discard the flavor pack
  • 1/2 lb other bean
  • 1 lb smoked sausage, sliced

Steps

  1. Wash and sort beans.
  2. Add beans and sliced sausage to pot with 8 c water.
  3. Add spices.
  4. Simmer for 2 hours or until done.
  5. Serve over rice.

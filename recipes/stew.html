Stew

Description

This stew is filled with meat, vegetables, and beans. Change the amount you'd like to make by adjusting the amounts and ratios of the ingredients. Dump whatever you want in!

Ingredients

  • 1 to 3 lbs of beef stew meat in chunks
  • 3 tbs oil
  • 1/3 c flour
  • 6 c beef stock
  • 1 head of cauliflower
  • 1 lbs carrots, sliced
  • 3-5 stalks celery, sliced (optional)
  • 1 lb frozen corn
  • 2 cans chick peas
  • 1 to 2 lbs mushrooms
  • Spices/seasonings: garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, thyme
  • Corn starch to thicken

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot on med-high to high heat.
  2. Dredge stew meat in flour and brown in batches.
  3. When meat is browned on each side, remove meat, deglaze pan with water.
  4. Add stock, cans of chick peas, and spices/seasoning.
  5. Simmer at least 1 hour.
  6. Add cauliflower and corn. Bring to simmer again. Cook for 45 minutes.
  7. If stew if thin, add cornstarch dissolved in water. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. If using peas, add and turn heat off.

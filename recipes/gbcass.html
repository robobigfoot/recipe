Green Bean Casserole with Chicken

Description

This is like a side dish, but it's the main dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 to 2 lbs chicken, diced
  • 4 cans cut green beans
  • 2 to 3 cans condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 c milk
  • Large bag fried onions

Steps

  1. Cook chicken well in skillet.
  2. Combine all green bean casserole ingredients in large bowl.
  3. Add chicken.
  4. Put mixture in greased 9x13 baking dish
  5. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
  6. Top with some fried onions, bake for 10 minutes more.

Stir Fry

Description

This is only kind of a stir fry.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb smoked sausage
  • 8 oz sliced carrots
  • 4 stalks celery, sliced
  • 8 oz shredded cabbage
  • Other cut vegetables of your choice

Steps

  1. Heat 1/4 c oil in wok on high.
  2. Add sausage and stir until starting to crisp.
  3. Add carrots and celery, stir until slightly soft.
  4. Add cabbage and stir until cooked.
  5. Serve over rice or noodles.

Pasta

Description

A simple noodle and sauce recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 large can crushed tomatos
  • 1 jar of tomato sauce
  • 1 lb pasta
  • Seasonings: Fennel, garlic powder, onion powder, basil, oregano

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in large pot. When half browned, add fennel seeds.
  2. When meat is cooked through, add crushed tomato and pasta sauce. Simmer.
  3. Boil water and cook pasta.
  4. Serve.

Teriyaki Chicken

Description

This has pineapples in it.

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 lbs chicken
  • 1 to 2 c Kikoman teriyaki sauce
  • 2 cans sliced pineapples

Steps

  1. Add chicken, teriyaki, and 1 can of pineapples, including juice, to crockpot.
  2. Cook on low for 8 hrs or high for 4.
  3. Serve with rice.
  4. Alternatively, prepare as above, but cook in oven for 2 hours, covered, until done.
